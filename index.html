What is Distrito Federal?

Welcome to Distrito Federal — a hidden gem of the Bay Area. Founded in 2022 by Issac Barrera, Distrito Federal has quickly become the go-to destination for traditional mexican cuisine, signature craft cocktails, and immersive educational experiences such as Taste of Tequila. A true hospitality experience is defined by more than just great food and drinks — it’s about creating a inviting atmosphere, delivering top-shelf service, and showcasing professional expertise in every way. Distrito Federal brings all three of these necessities together in one top-notch establishment.

Cuisine

At Distrito Federal, we take pride in offering a truly memorable take on elevated, traditional, Mexican cuisine. From our signature Enchiladas to our Specialty Tacos, every dish is crafted with care, superb flavor, and authenticity. Our menu is curated and executed by Chef Geo; Chef has been written about in multiple news articles due to his passion in bringing bold, vibrant Mexican flavors to food. He ensures that every plate offers something more than meets the eye marking the perfect balance of tradition, creativity, and taste.

Bar

Since Distrito’s inception, our goal has been to craft extraordinary drinks using the freshest ingredients. As a standard, we process all juices in-house and prepare each cocktail with professional precision and care. Creating specialty drinks is our passion, and each one is designed to leave you with a memorable experience you’ll want to revisit. Some of our most popular selections include the Lucid Dream—a clarified pear and matcha cocktail; 24 Carrot—a tequila-based drink featuring freshly juiced carrot and habanero-infused honey; and our signature Mexican Carajillo, a beloved favorite among guests and staff alike. Reserve a spot or stop by today!
